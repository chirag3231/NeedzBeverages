Who We Are

Needz Beverages provides beverage consultation solutions to restaurant beverage program, bar entrepreneurs, and established brand owners across Canada.

Creating a successful bar program goes beyond an innovative cocktail menu. Expertise in the trends and practices of the hospitality industry is vital to the success of a beverage program of a new or existing bar. At Needz, we have extensive experience in developing every aspect of inventive beverage programs that not only follow current trends, but create new ones that, in turn, lead to a successful business.
